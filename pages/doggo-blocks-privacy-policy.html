Overview

Doggo Blocks ("the App") is a casual puzzle game. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

Your high score is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data is not transmitted to any server.

Your Rights

The App is rated for all ages and does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children.
